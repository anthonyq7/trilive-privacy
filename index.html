Privacy Policy

How TriLive collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our real-time transit app for Portland's TriMet system.

Last updated August 10, 2025

TriLive is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency regarding how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. This Privacy Policy describes our practices when you use our mobile application ("TriLive") which provides real-time bus and train arrival information for TriMet public transit in the Portland, Oregon metropolitan area.

By using TriLive, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate and improve TriLive's features:

Location Data

  • Collected only with your consent when you enable location services.
  • Used to display nearby stops, calculate arrival times, and show relevant route information.
  • Processed in real time on your device; not stored after your session ends.

Usage Data and Device Information

  • Includes device model, operating system version, app version, and anonymized usage statistics.
  • Helps us diagnose crashes, improve performance, and optimize features.
  • Collected via third-party analytics and crash reporting tools (e.g., Apple Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics).

Push Notification Identifiers

  • If you enable notifications, we store a device-specific token to deliver arrival alerts.
  • Not linked to personally identifiable information.

We do not collect personal identifiers such as your name, email address, phone number, or payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide core functionality, including location-based stop searches and live arrival times
  • Deliver time-sensitive notifications for upcoming bus/train arrivals
  • Monitor app performance and troubleshoot technical issues
  • Improve user experience through usage analytics
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

3. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share limited data in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted vendors that help us deliver app functionality (e.g., push notification services, analytics platforms). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the specified purposes.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, regulation, or valid legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of TriLive, our users, or the public.
  • Aggregated or Anonymized Data: We may share non-identifiable usage statistics for research, reporting, or service improvement purposes.

4. Data Retention

  • Location Data: Not stored after the session ends.
  • Push Notification Tokens: Retained only while notifications are enabled on your device.
  • Analytics Data: Retained in aggregated, anonymized form to track performance trends.

We will delete or anonymize your data when it is no longer needed for the purposes described above.

5. Your Privacy Choices

You can manage your privacy settings at any time:

  • Location Access: Disable in your device settings to prevent location data collection.
  • Notifications: Disable in app or device settings to stop receiving alerts.
  • Data Deletion Requests: Contact us using the information below to request removal of stored push notification tokens.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to safeguard your information against unauthorized access, use, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of data transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

TriLive is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when the latest changes were made. Continued use of the App after changes are posted means you accept the revised policy.
